About Me

My name is Kim Le. I'm a UCF alumni with a bachelor degree in hospitality management. I been working in the service industry for 8 years. My most recent job was a restaurant manager for Disney. It has been fun, but i realized, i'm really not a "people" person. So i switch gear and enter the world of coding. I hope i will be good at it. My goal is to be able to build applications that will assist with the daily struggle and improve quality of life.

Beside being a workaholic, I enjoy reading, hiking, discovering new food joints and cool bars. I also love to travel. I want to see and experience the different cultures and landscape of the world before it's gone forever (yes, global warming is a real!). Hopefully, a career in coding will afford me more freedom to travel.
